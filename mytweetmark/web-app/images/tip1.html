How to use mytweetmark?


mytweetmark allows you to share bookmarks, posts and blogs on twitter and facebook. We provide analytics to you that helps determine page views and traffic generated through Twitter tweets or Facebook feeds.


Create categories to store your bookmarks. Use proper hashtags to identify the content. When you create bookmarks, posts or blogs, we will add the hashtag to the tweets or feeds. Watch the page views grow and through analytics see which site your traffic is coming from.


You can turn tweets or feeds on/off based on your choice when to make your content public or private. Choose your custom hashtag carefully. Every tweet or feed we send on your behalf, will contain your custom hashtag. Please see wefollow.com for popular hashtags.


New feature, we will analyze your tweets and extract bookmarks & friends. These bookmarks and friends can be archived for future reference and shared with your friends on your profile. We can automagically create you a blog from your tweets.
